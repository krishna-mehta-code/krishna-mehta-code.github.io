Rules

A rule is something you must follow because the language demands it.
Breaking a rule almost always makes your program fail to work.

Best Practices

A best practice is something you should do.
It's the standard, recommended, or most reliable way of doing things.
If you don’t follow it, your code may still work, but it might be confusing, unexpected, or worse than the usual approach.

Warnings

A warning is something you should not do.
Doing it usually leads to bugs, weird behavior, or unexpected results.
